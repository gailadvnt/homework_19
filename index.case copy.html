Final Project
Problem Statement

Potential donors do not feel safe and confident in the legitimacy and effectiveness of the New Mom project in order to confidently donate to the non-profit.

Mothers visiting the New Mom project’s site lack trust and doubt that their personal information is protected while on the site.

Solution

How might we redesign a website that not only aligns seamlessly with the organizations mission but also provides and communicates an enhanced, secure and trustworthy, user friendly platform for donors and new mothers seeking support, resources and community

By communicating the organization accomplishments and their mission through the website. Including real-people reviews, articles, interviews and a registered charity number.

User Testing & Feedback

“Purpose of website is unclear, fonts are inconsistent”

“Complicated steps to apply for baby bundles, would be better to apply by form instead of emailing”

“Unclear contact information and hassle to contact people volunteering at New Mom Project for questions and inquiries”

“Donations page is unreliable, no additional information to donate securely”

Prototype

A Prototyped of the redesign was created. The prototyped features updated forms for trustworthy donations and easier applications, an improved navigation with delightful interactions, real content and updated stats and photos that display the organizations hard work

Prototype link: https://tinyurl.com/Figma-Prototype-NMP

Learnings

“It’s essential to trust everyone on your team”

“How users feel about a website effects how successful it is”
